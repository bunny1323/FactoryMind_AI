STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE: HYDRAULIC PUMP BEARING INSPECTION & REPLACEMENT
Document ID: SOP-MNT-R215-087
Target Family: Hyundai R215L Excavator
Estimated Downtime: 6.5 Hours
Required Tools: Dial Indicator, Hydraulic Jack, 24mm Torque Wrench, Shaft Alignment Laser, Accelerometer

1. PREPARATION AND SAFETY
- Park the excavator on level ground. Lower all attachments (boom, arm, bucket) to the ground.
- Shut down the engine and lock out the hydraulic controls. Lock out the main electrical isolator.
- Allow hydraulic oil temperature to drop below 40°C.
- Relieve residual pressure in the hydraulic circuits by bleeding the accumulator.

2. VIBRATION DIAGNOSIS & INSPECTION
- Check pump shaft axial and radial play using a dial indicator. Normal axial play should be between 0.02 mm and 0.08 mm. Radial play should not exceed 0.12 mm.
- Inspect the bearing casing for grease discoloration or metal shaving contamination.
- If radial play exceeds 0.15 mm or shaft runout is detected, the main pump bearings must be replaced.

3. REPLACEMENT PROCEDURE
- Disconnect suction and delivery hoses from the main pump (Kawasaki K3V112DT). Cap all open lines to prevent dust ingress.
- Support the pump weight using an overhead engine hoist or heavy-duty jack.
- Remove the main pump mounting bolts (M16 flange bolts, 8 pieces).
- Slide the pump assembly out of the engine flywheel housing.
- Open the pump front housing to access the shaft bearings. Remove the retaining snap ring.
- Using a bearing puller, extract the worn shaft bearing (part SP-BRG-215). Clean the housing socket.
- Press-fit the new bearing (SP-BRG-215) using a hydraulic press tool. Apply lubrication grease.
- Reassemble the pump casing and torque casing bolts to 85 Nm.
- Mount the pump back to the flywheel. Use a laser alignment tool to align the pump shaft to the engine flywheel shaft. Runout tolerance must be < 0.05 mm.
- Secure mounting bolts and torque to 145 Nm. Reconnect hydraulic lines.

4. POST-REPLACEMENT CALIBRATION & VERIFICATION
- Fill the pump housing with clean, filtered hydraulic oil (ISO VG 46).
- Start the engine in low idle. Bleed air from the pump regulator plug.
- Run engine at high idle (2000 RPM) and measure vibration. Ensure peak-to-peak amplitude is below 0.05 mm.
